Recently, the field of AI video modeling has ushered in a new model: Higgsfield AI. Unlike existing video generation models such as [Luma](https://lumalabs.ai/), [Runway](https://runwayml.com/), and [Keling](https://app.klingai.com/global/), [Higgsfield AI](https://higgsfield.ai/) uses preset lens templates, and currently only supports graph-generated videos, allowing users to upload a single image to generate complex footage, such as vehicle explosions and decompositions.

302.AI currently has access to the Higgsfield AI video generation model as well as [Higgsfield.AI’s Generate Ads video workflow](https://higgsfield.ai/ads), which provides a total of more than 70 single/mixed preset templates, for more information, read the table below:
